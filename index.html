How it works

Simple for you.
Reliable for your residents.

BasepointGrid handles every part of the EV charging operation. You sign a site license and we handle the rest — permanently.

01

We fund & install

BasepointGrid covers 100% of hardware and installation costs. ChargePoint CT4000 dual-port Level 2 chargers — commercial grade, built for long-term operation.

02

Residents charge

Your residents access charging through the ChargePoint network using their phone. No fobs, no keys. Billing is handled automatically per session.

03

You earn every month

You receive a monthly revenue share from every session charged at your property. Estimated $350–$600/month at a typical deployment. No management required on your end.
