Here's How It Works

We provide the charger at $0 equipment cost to you and manage all PG&E paperwork and permitting. We own the hardware, handle all repairs, and collect payments from residents automatically. Every month, we send you a check. Your only out-of-pocket piece is the property-side electrical conduit — and we connect you directly with the electrician, or help route it through PG&E's infrastructure funding program.

Why This Matters Now
1
Your residents already need this. 1 in 4 new cars your residents buy this year is electric. Right now they're driving miles to public chargers because your building doesn't have one.
